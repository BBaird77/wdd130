Protagonists

Owain Tesla

The youngest child and only son of Ozpin Tesla, Owain is both young and inexperienced in facing down the challenges of the world. However, he strides forward seeking to help others with everything he has. A white-haired teen with a mischievous grin, he was sent with his sister to repay a debt.

Andraste Tesla-Noire

The older sister to Owain, and one of Ozpin’s daughters. She is an honest and smart young woman, who was sent by her father to pay back a favor that her family owes.

Jack Greil

The previous famed hero of Regalia, who was defeated several years ago in a duel with Rael’s champion, the Black Knight. He was a sandy haired young man in his early twenties when he fell.

Antagonists

Vladimir Volt

A powerful ice mage who fell from grace and became an immensely powerful vampire. He now serves as the primary foe of Regalia, and leads the armies of Rael against all in his path.

The Black Knight

A mysterious knight in jet black armor who wields a heavy sword and serves as the champion for the army of Rael.

Valen Kron

King of Rael, Hero of Light, and now feared as his armies raze the surrounding nations at his orders.
